Our mission

Greenhouse is working to address the climate emergency.

A respected and trusted voice on energy efficiency and clean energy solutions, we continue to work towards a smart, decarbonised, decentralised energy system.

Our values

We are driven by a fierce belief in the critical importance of our work.

We know we can make a difference. We understand the scale of the challenge but will never give up. Our skill, impartiality and professionalism mean we are always fully prepared– and trusted – when it comes to doing the right thing.

Our story

We’ve been a constant source of advice and support.

Our initial role was to work with the energy companies. Since then, we’ve seen governments steadily move towards low-carbon and renewable energy.
